Jakub Krukar visited Future Cities Lab Singapore and gave a talk on "Ecological Validity of Architectural Cognition". [Future Cities Laboratory (FCL) Global](https://fcl.ethz.ch) is a research collaboration between ETH Zurich and the Singapore universities with support from the National Research Foundation (NRF). It operates under the auspices of the Singapore-ETH Centre (SEC).

The [Architectural Cognition in Practice](https://futurecitieslab.world/module/ACP) team concentrates or bringing cognitive research into architectural workflows.
