SPARC and [SIL](https://sil.uni-muenster.de) have jointly hosted a workshop on "3D SketchMaps". The project, in which we partner with [Chair of Geoinformation Engineering](https://gis.ethz.ch/en/) and [Chair of Cognitive Science](https://cog.ethz.ch) (both at ETH Zürich), is sponsored by the Swiss National Science Foundation within their Sinergia programme focusing on "interdisciplinary, collaborative and breakthrough research".

The goal of the project is the extend the popular method for studying human memory of spatial environments - "sketch maps" - from a traditional paper-based 2D format to Virtual Reality-based 3D set-up. First publication describing the concept is already available [here](https://www.research-collection.ethz.ch/bitstream/handle/20.500.11850/565530/kim_cosit2022.pdf?sequence=1).
