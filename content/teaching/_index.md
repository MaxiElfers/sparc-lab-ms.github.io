# Lectures and Seminars

I teach Wayfinding and Navigation, Spatial Cognition, and Core Topic in GI Science courses.

My classes are interdisciplinary by design: I teach basic theories of Spatial Cognition and Cognitive Science by demonstrating how they are, or could be, applied by designers of buildings, cities, and spatial technologies.

I also lead the [Graduate School of Geoinformatics](https://www.uni-muenster.de/Geoinformatics/en/Studies/study_programs/PhD/) \- our structured PhD programme across all research labs of the Institute.
